For the best experience, please make sure that you have a working webcam and mic attached...

This is the Mouse Control Window, where you use the X/Y axis to control effects in the Effects Rack.

Note: by holding down the control (CTRL) key, you enable webcam gesture control and can control effects by curling your hand into a fist

This is the Effects Rack, where you can add effects to samples using the yellow effects buttons, and can control them using the X/Y axis in the Mouse Control Window

This is the Sample Window, where you select which samples you want to play and can addeffects to samples by clicking the yellow effects buttons and then clicking on an effect in the Effects Rack

Click the 'Clear FX' button to clear all effects while samples are still playing. Otherwise, click the 'Reset' button to reset everything back to their default settings

Here are four instruments provide different timbres depending on which osscilation shapes you choose. Pitch frequencies can be controlled in the Mouse Control Window by mouse or webcam gestures

These are Visualisers, which display the sample amplitude spectrum and waveform on the right-hand side and the amplitude of the Recording Studio on the left-hand side

This is the Recording Studio, where you can record and play back up to 4 of your own samples using a built-in or connected microphone

Click the 'Strobe' button for additional visualiser effects

Keyboard Shortcuts

Escape Key - press to reset all samples and effects back to default settings

Backspace - press to clear all effects while samples are still playing

Number and Letter Keys - press to toggle turning effects button on/off and sample playback (with space held down)

Rows correspond with the 4x4 Sample Window grid

Control Key (CTRL) - hold to activate webcam fist detection

Spacebar - hold to switch between toggling effects button on/off and sample playback
